Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Unpublished.pub?

Unpublished.pub is a platform where researchers can share unpublished drafts, unfinished projects, ideas, and data to find new collaborators or explore unpublished research work.

Is it free?

Yes. Unpublished.pub is completely free to use for all researchers.

What kind of content can I upload?

You can upload descriptions of your projects, drafts, or unfinished work. You can also include links to code repositories, datasets, or other supplementary materials that help explain or share your project. Make sure you have the rights to share any content you upload.

Can I collaborate with others on the platform?

Yes. You can contact other researchers through information provided on their published projects. We do not provide direct messaging; collaboration is arranged externally.

Who can see my projects?

Projects you publish on Unpublished.pub are visible to other registered users and visitors. You retain ownership of your content and can delete it at any time.

How do I create an account?

All accounts are created through ORCID login. Click “Sign in with ORCID” and authorize access. We only receive basic profile information (ORCID iD, public name, email if shared).

Do you store my ORCID password?

No. ORCID handles all authentication. We only receive the information you authorize us to access.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can request account deletion at any time. All associated user-generated content will be removed from the active system. Backups may retain data for a limited period for recovery purposes.

Who can I contact with questions?

You can contact us at: info@unpublished.pub.
