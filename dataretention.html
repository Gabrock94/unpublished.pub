Data Retention Policy

Last updated: 25/11/2025

This Data Retention Policy explains how long we retain personal and project-related information on our website (“we”, “our”, “us”).

1. Information We Retain

We collect and store the following information:

  • User Account Data: basic ORCID profile information (ORCID iD, public name, email if shared)
  • User-Generated Content: projects, drafts, ideas, and other content you submit
  • Security/Access Logs: minimal technical data (e.g., IP logs, authentication events) for security and fraud prevention

We do not retain any personal information beyond what is necessary for operating the site, as described in our Privacy Policy.

2. Retention Periods

Data Type Retention Period Notes
User Account Data Until account deletion Automatically removed upon user request, unless required by law
User-Generated Content As long as account is active or until user deletes content Content may remain in backups for a limited period
Security/Access Logs Up to 90 days Used for fraud prevention, troubleshooting, and security audits
Cookies (__cf_bm) ~30 minutes Essential for authentication; automatically expires

3. Deletion of Data

  • Account Deletion: Users may request deletion of their accounts and all associated content.
  • Automatic Removal: Data from deleted accounts is removed from active systems within a reasonable period. Backups may retain data for a short period for operational recovery.
  • Exceptions: Certain data may be retained to comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Service, or prevent fraud.

4. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to processing if applicable

To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@unpublished.pub

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Data Retention Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with a revised “Last updated” date.
