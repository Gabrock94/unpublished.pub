This Cookie Policy explains how this website (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, enhance security, and improve user experience. Some cookies are essential, meaning the website cannot operate correctly without them.

Cookies We Use

We use only one essential third-party cookie that is required for our authentication workflow:

Non-Essential Cookies

We do not use:

  • Analytics cookies
  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Preference or personalization cookies
  • Any other non-essential cookies

How to Manage Cookies

Because the only cookie we use is strictly necessary for security and authentication, it cannot be opted out of. If you disable all cookies through your browser, you will not be able to log in with ORCID.
