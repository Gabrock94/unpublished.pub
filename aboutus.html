About Us

Welcome to Unpublished.pub! Our mission is to make research more accessible, discoverable, and shareable, even when it is not formally published. We provide a platform where researchers can showcase drafts, unfinished projects, ideas, and data in order to find new collaborators and show scientific works which never reached the publication stage.

What We Do

We allow researchers to:

  • Share unpublished projects, ideas, and datasets.
  • Connect with other researchers for potential collaboration.
  • Increase visibility of work that is not formally published.

Our Philosophy

Science progresses faster when knowledge is shared openly. By giving researchers a safe space to share early-stage work, we hope to accelerate discovery, reduce duplication, and encourage collaboration across disciplines.
